Flexbox is a one-dimensional layout method for arranging items in rows or columns. Items flex (expand) to fill additional space or shrink to fit into smaller spaces. This article explains all the fundamentals.

Flex Box Use
1- display: flex;
2- flex-direction
3- flex-wrap
4- justify-content: start , end , center , space-around , space-between , space-evenly //justify-content main-axis pe hota i.e horizontal axis
5- align-items: start , end , center , stetch
6- order:{numebr} //Jiska number jitna badha hoga vo utna piche ayega. 
7- flex-shrink:{numer} eg - flex-shrink:3 \\iska mtlb hai ki element 4x se shrink karega 
8- flex-grow:{number}
